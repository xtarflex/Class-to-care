About Class to Care

Learn about our journey, our values, and the people driving our mission to improve healthcare across Nigeria.

Our Vision

To be the foremost promoter of access to high quality healthcare for all Nigerians.

We believe in a future where potential is limitless, inspiring us to build solutions that redefine what's possible for our communities.

Our Mission

What We Do
Improve healthcare service delivery across Nigeria.
How We Do It
Through continuous medical education, equipment donation, and promoting public health awareness.
For Whom
For healthcare practitioners, public institutions, and the communities they serve.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency in all our dealings.

Compassion

We put the communities we serve at the heart of every decision we make.

Excellence

We take responsibility for our results and constantly strive to improve our impact.

Our Journey

Tracing our milestones from a simple idea to an impactful organization.

2018

First Training Program

Held our first Basic Life Support (BLS) training, equipping 8 health workers from 3 local government areas with critical skills.

2019

First Equipment Donation

Donated a standing fan and a drug cart to the Ayomi Mafoluku Primary Health Centre, our first step in direct facility support.

2022

CAPRES in Schools

Launched the Cardio Pulmonary Resuscitation in Schools (CAPRES) project, starting with Div-Help schools in Oshodi, Lagos.

2023

OSATEA Online Training

Expanded our reach digitally with the OSATEA project, training over 100 nurses from the Naija Nurse Forum online.
