About Class to Care

Learn about our journey, our values, and the people driving our mission to improve healthcare across Nigeria.

Our Mission

To improve healthcare service delivery through continuous medical education for healthcare practitioners and promoting support to influence healthcare practice in public service.

Our Vision

To be the foremost promoter of access to high quality healthcare for all Nigerians.
