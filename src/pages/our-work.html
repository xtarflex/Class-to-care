Our Programs

Ongoing initiatives that form the backbone of our healthcare improvement efforts.

Continuous Medical Education

Regular workshops, seminars, and training sessions for healthcare professionals to stay updated with the latest medical practices and technologies.

  • Basic Life Support (BLS) Training
  • Emergency Response Protocols
  • Patient Care Best Practices
  • Medical Equipment Usage

Health Centre Support

Comprehensive support for primary health centers including equipment donation, infrastructure improvement, and staff training.

  • Medical Equipment Provision
  • Infrastructure Assessment
  • Staff Capacity Building
  • Supply Chain Support

Community Health Awareness

Public health campaigns and community outreach programs to educate people about preventive care and healthy lifestyles.

  • Health Education Campaigns
  • Preventive Care Workshops
  • Community Health Screenings
  • Youth Health Programs
