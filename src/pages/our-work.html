Our Work

Continuous Medical Education

We bridge the gap in healthcare delivery by providing hands-on training, mentorship, and continuous professional development for healthcare practitioners across Nigeria.

Our goal is to ensure every nurse and doctor is equipped with current, life-saving skills.

The OSATEA Project

Online Skills Advancement training for over 100 nurses on Airway Management and Oxygen Therapy.

BLS Workshops

Physical Basic Life Support (BLS) training for health workers across 3 Local Government Areas.

ACLS Training

Advanced Cardiac Life Support certification for specialized emergency response scenarios.

Equipment Donation & Support

Quality healthcare requires functional infrastructure. We assess the needs of Primary Health Centres (PHCs) and donate critical medical equipment.

We actively visit and rate health facilities to identify gaps in resources and direct our support where it is needed most.

Shogunle PHC Support

Donated digital thermometers, a modern drug cart, and office furniture to improve patient care.

Ayomi Mafoluku PHC

Provided essential facility upgrades including cooling systems (standing fans) and medical trolleys.

Public Health Awareness

Prevention is better than cure. We actively engage communities, schools, and the general public to raise awareness about critical health issues.

From schools to marketplaces, we bring life-saving knowledge directly to the people.

CAPRES Project

CPR in Schools initiative training students at schools like Div-Help College to become the next generation of lifesavers.

Disease Awareness

Large-scale screenings and education, such as our World Hepatitis Day outreach serving over 600 beneficiaries.

"Restart a Heart" Day

Annual public demonstrations teaching the community the fundamentals of CPR.

Improving healthcare and service delivery through continuing medical education for healthcare practitioners and promoting support to influence healthcare practice.
